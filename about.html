About Me

I moved to Perth from the Philippines in November 2020 to be with my husband, and that change opened the door to a new career path for me. In 2021, I joined a software testing bootcamp, which led to my first role as a test analyst and eventually to where I am now—as a Functional Test Engineer at Racing and Wagering Western Australia (RWWA). It’s been a journey of learning, growing, and always trying to make things better. Outside of work, I love spending time with my family, especially my 2-year old daughter, as well as calling family back home. I joined She Codes to have a better understanding of software development, and enhance my technical skills.
